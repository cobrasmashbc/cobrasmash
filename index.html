The Cobra Story: Our Milton Keynes Club

We are a dynamic and dedicated group of passionate badminton players from Milton Keynes, united by a shared love for the exhilarating sport of badminton. Our journey struck its first shuttle in January 2025. Since then, we've consistently hit the courts at the National Badminton Centre (NBC) every Wednesday, fostering a friendly and social community built on sportsmanship, continuous skill enhancement, and pure enjoyment. We are primarily an intermediate-level club, focusing on enjoyable yet competitive games. Our regular sessions are a testament to our collective commitment to the game and to the strong bonds we've forged as a team.

Our Club's Ambitions

We're not just about friendly games; we have exciting plans to grow and elevate Cobra Smash Badminton Club to new heights...

  • League Participation: Aiming to join and make our mark in the MKDBA (Milton Keynes & District Badminton Association) local leagues in the 2025 or 2026 season.
  • Annual Club Events: Planning internal badminton tournaments and fantastic social events throughout the year.
  • Sharpening Our Fangs: Friendly matches with other local badminton groups to hone our skills.
